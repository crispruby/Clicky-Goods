# Clicky Goods
A Unity‑built 3D arcade reaction game featuring three fast‑paced levels. Your goal is to click the good objects—Beer Barrels, 8‑Balls, and Cookies—before they hit the ground to earn points. But be careful: clicking a skull will decrease your score, and letting a good object fall off the screen ends the game instantly.

## Gameplay Overview 🚀
Clicky Goods challenges your speed and accuracy across multiple levels. Good objects fall from above, and you must click them quickly to score points before they reach the ground. Skulls also fall, but clicking them will reduce your score, so timing and precision are essential. If even one good object falls below the screen, the game ends. Each level increases the difficulty, creating a fast‑paced arcade experience.

## Controls 🎮
Mouse Cursor — Aim

Left Mouse Button — Click objects

## Features ✨
Clicky Goods includes three progressively challenging levels designed to test your reaction time and accuracy. The game features a variety of falling objects, including both helpful and harmful items, creating a fast‑paced arcade experience. Players must balance speed with careful decision‑making to avoid clicking skulls while still clearing good objects before they fall.
